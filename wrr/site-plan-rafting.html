Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to help those who are interested in rafting around the Boise area. We provide information about locations where our audience can raft as well as other valuable information to make their trip on the waters great.

Audience

Our audience is those who are looking to raft in the Boise area. We will be able to provide them with the information they need to have a successful trip, as well as to learn about the water features in the Boise, Idaho area. Our target customers are nature enthusiasts who are new to the area or looking to learn more. This website will be able to be accessed from any device.
